Forest sharks are said to inhabit dense, misty woodlands, thriving in ancient, untouched forests where sunlight barely reaches the ground. They are most commonly found in deep jungles, overgrown swamps, and shadowy evergreen forests, where their camouflaged, mossy skin helps them blend seamlessly into the surroundings.
